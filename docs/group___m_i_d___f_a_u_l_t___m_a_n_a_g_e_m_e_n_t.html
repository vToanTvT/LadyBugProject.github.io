Detailed Description

Fault detection, classification, and management system.

This module provides comprehensive fault protection including over-voltage, under-voltage, over-current, short-circuit, and over-temperature protection for both LLC and PFC stages. Features debounce filtering, configurable thresholds, auto-recovery/latch modes, and fault history logging.
